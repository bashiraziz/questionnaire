Understanding the Stages

Precontemplation: Indicates a lack of awareness that a problem exists or a reluctance to change.
Contemplation: Shows you are thinking about your problem and considering the possibility of change.
Action: Reflects that you are actively taking steps and working hard to change the problem.
Maintenance: Indicates you have made changes and are focused on preventing a relapse.
